USA Spending Data

Our application uses the US Government Spending dataset which has information about government awards or contracts. Our goal with this application is to empower university students and policy researchers with some key insights into budget allocation, different projects and their progress, and any impact of COVID-19 on government spending.

Our key users consist of university students and policy researchers who will highly benefit from this data because of the in-depth analysis. Both can explore the real-world spending data and use it for their analysis, understanding or just curiosity. Policy makers can use it for their policy analysis benefiting their decision making process.
